Base64 plugin

You can insert an image in base64 in src attribute of img tag.

Setup

        
<-- At the bottom of your BODY, AFTER jQuery and Trumbowyg imports -->
<script src="bower_components/trumbowyg/dist/plugins/base64/trumbowyg.base64.js"></script>
